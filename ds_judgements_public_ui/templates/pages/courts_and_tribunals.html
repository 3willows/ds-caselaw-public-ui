Judgments and decisions by court or tribunal

The UK courts and tribunal system is like a legal hierarchy. The Supreme Court is at the top, dealing with the final appeals. Below that, there are different courts for various legal issues, such as the Court of Appeal and the Crown Court. Specialised tribunals handle specific problems like employment or immigration. Overall, these courts and tribunals are essential for making fair legal decisions and resolving different types of issues.
